Our Story

Founded in 2007, Smart Freight Express Shipping and Logistics has established itself as a premier global logistics provider. With over 17 years of industry expertise, we've built a reputation for reliability, innovation, and customer-centric service.

What started as a small freight forwarding company has grown into an international logistics powerhouse, serving clients across 150+ countries worldwide. Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has made us a trusted partner for businesses of all sizes.
